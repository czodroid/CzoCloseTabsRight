# Czo Close Tabs Right

## General

The Czo Close Tabs Right extension adds a context menu option to
browser tabs to close tabs to the right of the selected one

## Information being collected

The developer of the Czo Close Tabs Right extension does not collect any
personal information about users. The identity of users of this
extension is completely unknown.
